About Us

About Us

At The Exponent Schools, we welcome you to a hub of quality education and high moral standards. Our journey began on September 16th, 2002, as Al-Fatiu International School in Orile Iganmu, Lagos, founded by two parties who parted ways amicably.

Thanks to the support of parents and community leaders, we expanded to owning an acre of land in Badagry and a building with 12 classrooms in Iledu, Lagos, after nine years of operation as a primary school. Our commitment to providing younger generations with a diverse-based education through an enriching curriculum and high-quality teaching has been the driving force behind our decision to establish a secondary school.

Alhamdulillah, The Exponent Schools has successfully nurtured several students from early education to securing their Secondary School Certificates (WASSCE) over the past 20 years. Our founding principles have remained constant throughout our growth journey, even as we continue to make significant contributions in the education space with our serene learning environment, standard classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, and other facilities.
